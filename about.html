About Us

HJ Cruz Electrical is led by Paul Palazzolo, a licensed electrician and electrical engineer with over 25 years of experience across commercial, industrial, and rail infrastructure projects in Sydney. Paul has worked extensively with organisations such as Sydney Trains, Sydney Metro, Transport for NSW and major commercial clients.

Qualifications & Accreditation

  • Advanced Diploma of Electrical Engineering
  • NSW Electrical Contractor Licence
  • Engineers Australia – Associate Member
  • FPAS – Emergency & Exit Lighting
  • WHS White Card, First Aid, CPR, LV Rescue

Capabilities

Our capability covers electrical design review, project and contract management, maintenance planning, emergency and exit lighting, switchboard upgrades, fault finding, and compliance with Australian Standards and client requirements.
